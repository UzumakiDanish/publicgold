What is Public Gold?

Public Gold is a reputable company specializing in the trade of gold and silver bullion in Malaysia. Founded in 2008, it offers products like gold bars, dinar coins, and silver bars. Public Gold is known for its Shariah-compliant investment options, making it a trusted choice for halal investments.

  • Wide range of products including gold and silver bullion.
